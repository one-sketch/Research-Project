Influence on Public Opinion

Shaping Views

Commentary channels can influence public opinion by presenting information with their own interpretations. Research in First Monday examined YouTube's role in politics and current events, finding that alternative news channels attract significant attention and often act as citizen journalists or political commentators.

Echo Chambers and Polarization

YouTube's recommendation algorithms can create echo chambers, where users mostly see content that aligns with their existing beliefs, potentially deepening societal divisions. A study in SAGE Open introduced the Toxicity Polarization Score to measure polarization in YouTube comments, suggesting that certain content fosters unbalanced and toxic discussions.
