Ethical Concerns

Show more

Some commentary channels are criticized for creating toxic environments or engaging in online harassment under the guise of critique.

An analysis on arXiv examined the monetization of controversial content, finding that while such content can increase engagement, it often correlates with higher toxicity, raising ethical questions about balancing monetization and responsible content creation.

Impact on Mental Health

Show more

The pressure to stay relevant and deal with public attention can take a toll on creators' mental health.

MrBeast has admitted that focusing on his well-being might have slowed his success.

Research from Griffith University also shows that frequent YouTube use is linked to higher levels of loneliness, anxiety, and depression, affecting both creators and viewers.
